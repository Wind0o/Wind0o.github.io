Common Questions

Why does Somio ask for Photos access?
Somio needs Photos access to browse the photos, videos, GIFs, and Live Photos you authorize.

Does Somio upload my photos or videos?
No. Somio is designed as a local-first browsing app and does not upload your media to Somio servers.

How do I manage Somio Pro?
Somio Pro purchases and subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and the App Store subscription settings.

Where are the terms?
Somio uses the Apple standard End User License Agreement: Terms of Use (EULA).

常见问题

为什么 Somio 需要相册权限？
Somio 需要相册权限，才能浏览你授权范围内的照片、视频、GIF 和实况照片。

Somio 会上传我的照片或视频吗？
不会。Somio 是本机优先的浏览工具，不会把你的媒体内容上传到 Somio 服务器。

如何管理 Somio Pro？
Somio Pro 购买和订阅通过 Apple ID 和 App Store 订阅设置管理。

使用条款在哪里？
Somio 使用 Apple 标准最终用户许可协议：使用条款（EULA）。
