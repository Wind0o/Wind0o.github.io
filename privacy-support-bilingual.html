隐私政策

Somio 是一款面向 iPhone 和 iPad 的本机照片与视频浏览应用。用户授权系统相册权限后，Somio 可帮助用户浏览 Apple 照片图库中的照片、视频、GIF 和 Live Photo。

Somio 不创建用户账号，不会将照片或视频上传到 Somio 服务器，不提供云同步，不包含广告 SDK，也不使用第三方分析 SDK。

照片与视频

Somio 仅访问用户通过 iOS 照片权限系统允许访问的照片和视频。浏览、收藏、本地标签、分享、删除请求、筛选以及飞行模式行为均在设备本地或用户授权范围内完成。

Somio 不运营云端媒体库，也不会把用户的照片图库内容发送到 Somio 服务器。

App 内购买

Somio Pro 购买和订阅由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。Somio 使用 Apple StoreKit 加载商品信息、处理购买、恢复购买并检查权益状态。支付信息由 Apple 处理，Somio 不收集用户的支付信息。

Somio 使用 Apple 标准最终用户许可协议：使用条款（EULA）。

数据收集

Somio 不会从本 App 收集个人数据。

本地数据

Somio 可能会在用户设备上保存应用设置、本地标签、播放或浏览状态，用于提供应用功能。这些信息保留在设备本地，不会发送到 Somio 服务器。

权限

照片图库权限用于浏览和管理用户选择或授权的内容。

网络访问可能用于 Apple StoreKit 商品加载和权益检查。

Somio 内的飞行模式开启后，会将浏览限制为本地已缓存或本地可用的媒体内容。

用户控制

用户可以随时在 iOS 设置中更改或撤销照片访问权限。用户可以通过删除 App 来删除本地应用数据。App Store 购买和订阅可通过用户的 Apple ID 订阅设置进行管理。

儿童隐私

Somio 并非面向儿童的应用，也不会故意收集儿童的个人数据。

变更

当 Somio 功能或 App Store 要求发生变化时，本政策可能会更新。最新版本将通过 App Store Connect 使用的公开政策链接提供。

Privacy Policy

Somio is a local photo and video browsing app for iPhone and iPad. The app helps users browse photos, videos, GIFs, and Live Photos from the Apple Photos library after the user grants system photo permission.

Somio does not create user accounts, does not upload photos or videos to Somio servers, does not provide cloud sync, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Photos and Videos

Somio accesses only the photos and videos that the user allows through the iOS Photos permission system. Browsing, favorites, local tags, sharing, deletion requests, filtering, and flight mode behavior are handled on the device and within the permissions granted by the user.

Somio does not operate a cloud media library and does not send the user's photo library content to Somio servers.

In-App Purchases

Somio Pro purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Somio uses Apple StoreKit to load product information, handle purchases, restore purchases, and check entitlement status. Payment information is handled by Apple and is not collected by Somio.

Somio uses the Apple standard End User License Agreement: Terms of Use (EULA).

Data Collection

Somio does not collect personal data from this app.

Local Data

Somio may store app settings, local tags, and local playback or browsing state on the user's device to provide app functionality. This information remains local to the device and is not sent to Somio servers.

Permissions

Photo Library permission is used to browse and manage content selected by the user.

Network access may be used for Apple StoreKit product loading and entitlement checks.

Flight mode inside Somio limits browsing to local cached or locally available media when enabled.

User Control

Users can change or revoke photo access in iOS Settings at any time. Users can delete local app data by deleting the app from their device. App Store purchases and subscriptions can be managed through the user's Apple ID subscription settings.

Children's Privacy

Somio is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when Somio changes its features or App Store requirements change. The latest version will be made available through the public policy link used in App Store Connect.
