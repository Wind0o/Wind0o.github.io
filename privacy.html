Somio Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

Somio is a local photo and video browsing app. It helps you rediscover photos, videos, GIFs, and Live Photos from the photo library you authorize on your device.

Somio does not require an account, does not include advertising SDKs, does not include analytics SDKs, and does not upload your photos or videos to Somio servers.

Data Collection

Somio does not collect personal data from this app. Your photo and video content remains on your device or in Apple Photos/iCloud Photos under Apple's system photo library behavior.

Photos Access

Somio requests access to your photo library only so the app can display, favorite, mark, share, export, or delete items that you choose to browse. You can limit or revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings.

Local Storage

Somio may store local app settings, local marks, queue state, and cache metadata on your device. These are used to keep the browsing experience smooth and private. This information is not sent to Somio servers.

iCloud Photos

If an item is stored in iCloud Photos and is not fully available on the device, Apple's Photos framework may download that item when you view, export, or share it. Somio does not operate a separate cloud media library.

Purchases

Somio Pro purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. Somio uses StoreKit to read purchase status and unlock Pro features. Payment information is processed by Apple, not Somio.

Somio uses the Apple standard End User License Agreement: Terms of Use (EULA).

Somio 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 5 月 31 日

概览

Somio 是一个本机照片和视频浏览工具，用于在你授权的系统相册范围内浏览照片、视频、GIF 和实况照片。

Somio 不要求账号，不接入广告 SDK，不接入分析 SDK，也不会把你的照片或视频上传到 Somio 服务器。

数据收集

Somio 不会从此 App 中收集个人数据。你的照片和视频内容保留在设备本地，或按照 Apple 照片/iCloud 照片的系统行为保存在 Apple 服务中。

相册权限

Somio 请求相册权限，只用于展示、收藏、标记、分享、导出或删除你选择浏览的内容。你可以随时在 iOS 设置中限制或撤销相册权限。

本机存储

Somio 可能在设备本地保存 App 设置、本机标记、队列状态和缓存元数据，用于保持浏览体验顺畅和私密。这些信息不会发送到 Somio 服务器。

iCloud 照片

如果内容存放在 iCloud 照片且设备本地没有完整文件，Apple 的照片框架可能会在你查看、导出或分享该内容时按需下载。Somio 不运营独立云端媒体库。

购买

Somio Pro 购买和订阅由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。Somio 使用 StoreKit 读取购买状态并解锁 Pro 功能。付款信息由 Apple 处理，不由 Somio 处理。

Somio 使用 Apple 标准最终用户许可协议：使用条款（EULA）。
